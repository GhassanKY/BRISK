**Brisk** is an easy-to-use, powerful Mac utility designed to help you perform **complete uninstalls** of your applications and reclaim valuable storage space. When you simply drag an app to the Trash, it often leaves behind hidden caches, preferences, and leftover files. Brisk hunts down and safely removes all of these unnecessary files.

## ✨ Why use Brisk?

* **Free Up Space**: Reclaim gigabytes of storage on your Mac that are taken up by forgotten files.
* **Complete Uninstalls**: Confidently delete applications knowing that absolutely no junk data is left behind.
* **Deep Leftover Scanner**: Intelligently scans your Mac to find orphaned files from apps you deleted months or even years ago.
* **Fast & Beautiful**: Designed to be incredibly fast and looks great in both Light and Dark modes.
* **100% Safe**: Built-in safeguards ensure that critical macOS system files are never touched.

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

Brisk is built following modern macOS engineering standards to ensure stability and speed:

* **Pattern**: Clean **MVVM** (Model-View-ViewModel) architecture.
* **Language**: Native **Swift** for maximum performance.
* **SDK**: Targeting latest macOS environments for modern feature support.

## 🚀 How to Install

1. Download the latest version of Brisk from the **[Releases page](#)**. *(Link to be updated when released)*
2. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
3. Drag the **Brisk** app icon into your **Applications** folder.
4. Open your Applications folder and double-click Brisk to launch it!

## 📖 How to Use Brisk

1. **Select an App to Uninstall**: The sidebar displays all the applications currently installed on your Mac. Click on any app to see all its associated hidden files.
2. **Remove Items**: Click the red **"Remove Items"** button. Brisk will safely batch-delete all the selected files for you. *(Brisk may ask for your Mac administrator password once to securely delete protected files)*.
3. **Scan for Old Leftovers**: If you don't have a specific app selected, simply click **"Scan Now"** on the main screen. Brisk will scan your entire system for old leftovers from apps you previously trashed.

> **Note on Permissions**: For Brisk to find all hidden leftovers, it needs permission to look at your system. If prompted, please open your Mac's System Settings, go to **Privacy & Security > Full Disk Access**, and enable the switch for Brisk.
